The ZARIA Origin

Founded in the heart of Durgapur, West Bengal, ZARIA began with a simple observation: finding the perfect gift was often stressful, and the presentation rarely matched the sentiment inside.

We set out to change that by curating premium, handcrafted items from local artisans and presenting them in our signature deep wine boxes. What started as a small project for friends and family quickly blossomed into a luxury brand dedicated to the fine art of gifting.

Today, we proudly ship our curated hampers across India, ensuring that whether it's a grand wedding or a quiet anniversary, your loved ones feel truly celebrated.
